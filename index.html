About Us

V fit women's gym

V Fit Women's Gym is a dedicated fitness centre exclusively for women, located in the heart of Attur, Tamil Nadu. With a warm, welcoming environment and expert guidance, we help women of all fitness levels achieve their health and wellness goals — whether you are just starting your fitness journey or looking to push your limits further. Our gym is equipped with modern facilities and staffed by experienced trainers who understand the unique needs of women.

Rated 4.9 out of 5 by our members, V Fit is more than just a gym — it is a community. We open as early as 5:00 AM to accommodate your busy schedule, six days a week, and offer Sunday morning sessions as well. Located conveniently at GM Home, 78/70, Arunagirinathar Street, Attur, we are committed to making fitness accessible, enjoyable, and effective for every woman who walks through our doors.
